What is mental health?

Mental health is the wellbeing of a person socially, physically, and emotionally. It influences how we act and react, bad mental health can result in poor self confidence and worth, and thoughts of hurting yourself and others.

Several indications of poor mental health include;

  • Poor eating habits
  • Little to no energy
  • Mood swings
  • Substances abuse
  • Thoughts of hurting yourself or others

What is the point of this website?

The point of this website is to provide easy accessibility to mental help resources for teenagers. So instead of having to search for help, this website provide links to help pages and websites along with a summary of what these websites are.
